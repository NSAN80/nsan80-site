Ideas

This section is for the broader thinking: what AI can become, how collaboration changes creation, and why practical imagination matters.

AI as infrastructure

AI should become more than novelty. It can become part of how companies reason, automate, design, analyze, and operate.

Humans still matter

Direction, judgment, correction, taste, and experience remain essential. AI expands the build space, but humans still decide what is worth building and why.

Evolution over perfection

The most interesting systems are often not fully planned at the start. They evolve through iteration, correction, surprise, and experimentation.
